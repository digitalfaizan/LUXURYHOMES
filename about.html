We Started With a Simple Belief

In 2012, our founder Sahil noticed a problem: most property dealers in Indirapuram were transactional. They'd show you a property, take a commission, and disappear. Buyers were left with undisclosed legal issues, hidden costs, and no support.

Luxury Homes was built to be different. We started as a two-person team in Gyan Khand 1, and grew by word-of-mouth to become Indirapuram's highest-rated property firm — because we genuinely cared about outcomes, not just transactions.

Today we serve buyers, sellers, investors and landlords across Indirapuram, Crossings Republik and Vasundhara. Every listing in our portfolio is physically verified, legally cleared, and RERA compliant before it reaches you.
